Adding & Subtracting Polynomials

    

Example 1:
  Simplify: ((5h^3)-8h)+((-2h^3)-(h^2)-2h)
  Rewrite without parentheses:
   (5h^3)-8h+(-2h^3)-(h^2)-2h
  Group like terms:
   (5h^3)-(2h^3)-(h^2)8h-2h
  Simplify:
   (3h^3)-(h^2)-10h

Example 2:
    Simplify: ((5h^3)-8h) - ((-2h^3)-(h^2)-2h)
    Rewrite without parentheses:
      (5h^3)-8h-(-2h^3)-(-h^2)-(-2h)
    Group like terms and simplify:
      (5h^3)+(2h^3)+h^2-8h+2h
    Simplify:
      (7h^3)+(h^2)-6h
