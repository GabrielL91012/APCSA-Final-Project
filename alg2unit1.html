Adding & Subtracting Polynomials

    

Example 1:
  Simplify: ((5h^3)-8h)+((-2h^3)-(h^2)-2h)
  Rewrite without parentheses:
   (5h^3)-8h+(-2h^3)-(h^2)-2h
  Group like terms:
   (5h^3)-(2h^3)-(h^2)8h-2h
  Simplify:
   (3h^3)-(h^2)-10h

Example 2:
    Simplify: ((5h^3)-8h) - ((-2h^3)-(h^2)-2h)
    Rewrite without parentheses:
      (5h^3)-8h-(-2h^3)-(-h^2)-(-2h)
    Group like terms and simplify:
      (5h^3)+(2h^3)+h^2-8h+2h
    Simplify:
      (7h^3)+(h^2)-6h
      
 

      
Multipying & Dividing Polynomials

      

Example 1:
    Simplify: (5x+3) * (6x+5)
    Rewrite into separate parts:
      (5x)(6x)+(3)(6x)+(5x)(5)+(3)(5)
    Evalutate terms and simplify:
      30x^2 + 43x + 15
        
Example 2:
    Simplify: (9x^2 + 6x + 5) / (3x+2)
    Set up synthetic long division:
           3x    
      3x+2|9x^2 + 6x +5
           -9x^2 - 6x
           0 + 5
    Answer: 3x + 5/(3x+2)
