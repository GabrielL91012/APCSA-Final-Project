Unit 3 --> Polynomial Factorization

      
Factoring Monomials

      

          This simply is a combination of the processes of unit 2. Use those to determine the answers for factoring. Make sure to find the GCF when factoring to factor to the smallest number possibly

          Examples:
          1) 45y^6 = (5y)(9y^5)
          2) GCF of 35y^7 and 7y^5 = 7y^5
        
     

     
Factoring Polynomials

    

          Same as factoring monomials except it is with more terms so simply find GCF of all terms

          Examples:
          1) 18x^7 + 36(x^3)(y^5) = (18x^3)(x^4 + y^5)
          2) GCF of 72(x^5) + 20 and 31x^3 + y^2 + 18 is x^2 + 2
    

      
Geometric Series

      

         A geometric series is a series that increases by multiplying each term by the same constant
         The formula for this is a + ar + ar2 + ar3 + ... in which r3 would be the ratio to the third power.
